Cookie preferences

Below is a breakdown of the different types of cookies used on this website along with switches to give you control of how and when they may be used by us. These switches will not affect third party cookies used on our website (see Third Party Cookies section below for more details).

Further details of all cookies used on our website can be found on our Cookie Policy

Essential

Some cookies are essential for the smooth running of our website. You may not be able to use our website without these cookies.

Analytics

In order to improve our website we anonymously track some user behaviours. This enables us to see how visitors use our website. It also allows us to see which features are most popular and make them better and remove the features that nobody uses. We would find it difficult to analyse how well our website was performing and improve it without these cookies.

User experience

Some cookies are used to improve the experience you have whilst on our website. These enable us to remember your preferences (like which language you want to view our website in) or remember you when you come back to our website. We'd recommend that you keep these switched on.

Interest-based advertising

To try to ensure that adverts we show you (on our website or websites of selected third parties) and, in some instances, email marketing campaigns we send you are relevant to your interests we use cookies to collect information about the types of things that interest you.
